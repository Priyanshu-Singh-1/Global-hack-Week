FAQs

Cats in Tech Guild - Global Hack Week, MLH

What is the Cats in Tech Guild?

chat_bubble_outline

The Cats in Tech Guild is a community for beginners created for Global Hack Week. It is hosted by Major League Hacking! We share knowledge and resources, and provide support for one another in our careers.

How do I join the guild?

chat_bubble_outline

You can join the guild by visiting our Global Hack Week website and filling out the registration application form. Once your application is submitted and approved, you'll be able to join our discord community and have access to all of our resources and events.

What kinds of events does the guild host?

chat_bubble_outline

Global Hack Week host a variety of events, including networking events, workshops, and many other challenges. We daily have some challenges, and also some cool projects to make!

I am not in the technology industry, can I still join the guild?

chat_bubble_outline

The guild is open to all tech and non tech lovers, regardless of your industry. However, the majority of our members are in the technology industry and our resources and events will be tailored to this audience. Ans yes, youc can still register to this event.

What are the benefits of joining the guild?

chat_bubble_outline

As a member of the guild, you'll have access to exclusive resources and events, as well as the ability to connect with other industry professionals in the technology industry. You'll also be able to share your own knowledge and resources with the community.
